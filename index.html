Against artificial intelligence in weapons: writings 1985 -- 87

Jon Jacky

Many years ago I wrote some articles and a long book chapter to explain -- and argue against -- proposals to develop artificial intelligence (AI) for military weapons. They are collected here out of personal and historical interest.

The Strategic Computing Program, in Computers in Battle: Will They Work edited by David Bellin and Gary Chapman, Harcourt Brace Jovanovich, 1987, pages 171 -- 208, 333 - 337.
